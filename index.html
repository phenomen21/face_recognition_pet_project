A face recognition pipeline demonstrated

Upload somebody's photo - the "Celeb-finder" will demonstrated what it takes to recognize a face on it

First, it will search a face on a picture - it takes a specially trained neural network to search for it

Next it will recognize certain points on the face: eyes, nose and mouth - this is the job for the second neural network

Next it will rotate and resize the face so the eyes and mouth will be on preset locations - no neural network here

Finally the third neural network will encode the face in a special series of numbers and it will look for the closest relatives it could find in its celebrities database, which is over 6.3k people

The Celeb-finder will show you the person it thinks is the closest match to a given face
